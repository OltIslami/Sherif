Career Journey

With over 25 years of experience in translation, interpretation, and project coordination, Sherif Zeqiri has established himself as a trusted professional in the international community. Specializing in legal, police, and educational matters, Sherif has worked with a wide range of organizations, including EU-funded projects, international missions, and local institutions in Kosovo.

Sherif's career began in the late 1990s as an interpreter for KFOR and OSCE, where he played a crucial role in facilitating communication between international forces and local authorities. Over the years, he has taken on increasingly responsible roles, including serving as a Senior Translation Officer and Office Manager for the Kosovo Educator Development Program (KEDP), where he organized large-scale educational conferences and study tours.

A court-certified translator and interpreter, Sherif is fluent in Albanian, Serbian, English, and Bosnian, with basic knowledge of German and Turkish. His ability to work in multicultural environments and his strong organizational skills have made him a key contributor to projects focused on education, law enforcement, and the rule of law in Kosovo.

Sherif is highly regarded for his leadership, communication, and teamwork skills, as well as his ability to manage complex projects under pressure. He continues to contribute to the development of Kosovo through his work with international organizations and his own translation business, Intertalk Translations.
